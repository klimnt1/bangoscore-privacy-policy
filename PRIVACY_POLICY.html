Privacy Policy for BangoScore

Last updated: December 22, 2025

Introduction

BangoScore ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information when you use BangoScore.

Data Collection

BangoScore does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

We do not:

  • Collect your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Track your location
  • Access your device's camera, microphone, or contacts
  • Collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Share any data with third parties

Data Storage

All game data (player names, scores, game history, and statistics) is stored locally on your device using your device's browser storage (LocalStorage). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Remains under your complete control

Internet Connection

BangoScore works completely offline and does not require an internet connection. The app does not make any network requests or communicate with any servers.

Third-Party Services

BangoScore does not integrate with or use any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services

Children's Privacy

BangoScore does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal information at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since BangoScore does not collect or store any personal information on our servers, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over all app data stored locally on your device.
